Key Initiatives

Green Energy & Solar Panels

We champion renewable energy by promoting the installation of solar panels across the island. This initiative reduces our carbon footprint, lowers energy costs, and nurtures a cleaner, greener environment.

Training Programmes for the Unemployed

Our extensive training programmes offer job readiness, vocational skills, and personal development courses to empower citizens and prepare them for emerging opportunities.

Empowering Homemakers

With flexible work initiatives and specialized training, homemakers are encouraged to step into professional roles, ensuring personal growth and financial independence.

Community Centre

Our vibrant community centre is the heart of local events, cultural gatherings, and public forums, fostering collaboration and civic pride.

Sports Centre

Equipped with state‑of‑the‑art facilities, our sports centre promotes health, teamwork, and active lifestyles with regular tournaments and fitness events.

Trash Pickup Dates
