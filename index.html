Welcome to ISLAND™

We are committed to fostering a vibrant, sustainable future for all citizens. Discover our initiatives, policies, and community programs below.

Our Policies

Green Energy

ISLAND™ is dedicated to sustainable development. Our new regulations encourage the use of solar panels to reduce our carbon footprint and ensure a cleaner environment.

Employment Support

We believe in empowering every individual to thrive. That’s why we offer a range of training programmes for unemployed citizens to help them gain valuable skills and secure meaningful work.

Encouraging Homemakers

Homemakers are encouraged to explore flexible work opportunities and training, ensuring everyone has a pathway to personal and professional growth.

Training & Development Programs

ISLAND™ invests in comprehensive training and development programs aimed at improving the employability of all citizens. From technical workshops to creative skill-building sessions, our programs are designed to nurture a dynamic workforce.

  • Job Readiness Training
  • Vocational Skill Workshops
  • Entrepreneurship Incubators
  • Technology & Innovation Seminars

Community Facilities

Community Centre

A hub for social gatherings, cultural events, and educational activities. Residents are encouraged to utilize the space for community-led projects and programs.

Sports Centre

Our modern sports centre is open to citizens of all ages, offering facilities for fitness, team sports, and recreational activities to promote a healthy lifestyle.
